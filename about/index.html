·About Africa 63

Built on trust. Rooted in Africa.

Africa 63 is a public affairs firm working at the intersection of government, business, and international development — helping institutions mobilise resources, structure public projects, and build the credibility that progress requires.

01Our Purpose

A Public Affairs Firm
for Africa's Development.

Africa 63 supports public and private sector development initiatives. From fundraising and resource mobilisation to public–private partnerships and governance reform, we help institutions deliver on their mandate.

Africa 63 facilitates the mobilisation of sustainable investments and the development of structured public projects. We provide legal advisory to commercial companies, promote leadership and good governance in public management, and deliver training programmes that strengthen institutional and operational capacity.

Above all, we facilitate collaboration — between governments, businesses, development partners, and the communities they serve. We believe the most durable outcomes are built not on a single transaction, but on relationships of trust maintained over time.

Africa 63 helps institutions improve their performance, efficiency, and sustainability — and we advise on building and maintaining the strong relationships that hold complex initiatives together.

02What We Believe

Principles that guide the work.

01

Trust First

Credibility is earned — through evidence, discretion, and consistency. We let the quality of the work, and the strength of our relationships, speak.

02

Partnership

We work alongside governments, businesses, and communities — never above them. Lasting progress is shared progress, built with the people it serves.

03

Sustainability

We build for performance, efficiency, and the long term — structuring projects and strengthening institutions so outcomes endure beyond us.
