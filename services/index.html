·Our Capabilities

What we do.

Seven capabilities, one discipline: Africa 63 turns public ambition into structured, credible, and sustainable outcomes — across government, enterprise, and international development.

01The Discipline

Every engagement begins with one question — how do we move this forward, sustainably? From that question, our capabilities follow.

01

Fundraising & Resource Mobilisation

↗

Public fundraising and sustainable investment for structured initiatives — connecting credible projects to the capital and partners that make them possible.
02

Sustainable Investment & Public Projects

↗

Facilitating sustainable investment and the development of structured public projects — shaping initiatives that are bankable, accountable, and built to deliver.
03

Public–Private Partnerships

↗

Designing and advancing PPP initiatives that align public objectives with private capability — partnerships that share risk fairly and create lasting value.
04

Legal Advisory

↗

Legal advisory services to commercial companies — helping enterprises navigate regulation, structure sound agreements, and operate with confidence.
05

Governance & Leadership

↗

Promoting leadership and good governance in public management — advising institutions on transparency, accountability, and effective decision-making.
06

Institutional & Operational Capacity

↗

Training programmes that strengthen institutional and operational capacity — equipping teams to perform, adapt, and sustain results over time.
07

Multi-Stakeholder Collaboration

↗

Facilitating collaboration between governments, businesses, development partners, and communities — building the coalitions that complex initiatives require.
02How We Engage

Deliberate. Evidence-led. Partnership-first.

01

Listen & Map

We begin by understanding the institution, the mandate, and the stakeholders — the full landscape before any recommendation.

02

Structure & Advise

We design the strategy, the structure, and the partnerships — grounded in evidence and built for accountability.

03

Deliver & Sustain

We support execution and strengthen capacity — so outcomes hold long after the engagement ends.
