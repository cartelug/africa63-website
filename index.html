01Who We Are

A consulting office
for Africa's
public future.

A63 supports public and private sector development across the continent. We mobilise sustainable investment, structure public projects, advance public–private partnerships, and strengthen the institutions that carry nations forward.

Founded on a single conviction — that Africa's progress is built through trust between governments, businesses, development partners, and the communities they serve — we work where strategy meets execution. From resource mobilisation to governance reform, we help institutions improve their performance, efficiency, and long-term sustainability.

Our approach is deliberate, evidence-led, and partnership-first. We do not chase headlines. We build the relationships, structures, and credibility on which lasting outcomes depend.

04Featured Engagement
Kinshasa · 2022

Embassy of Sweden
× LYREC

A63 supported the partnership between the Embassy of Sweden and LYREC — a platform of civil-society youth organisations in the Democratic Republic of the Congo — strengthening youth participation in governance for sustainable development.

Delivered under the LYREC–LSU partnership, the engagement equipped the platform to extend its work across Central Congo, Kwilu, and Central Kasaï — advancing citizen engagement in public governance on the ground.
