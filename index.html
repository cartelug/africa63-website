01Who We Are

A Consulting Office
for Africa's Public Future.

Africa 63 supports public and private sector development across the continent. We mobilise sustainable investment, structure public projects, advance public–private partnerships, and strengthen the institutions that carry nations forward.

Founded on a single conviction — that Africa's progress is built through trust between governments, businesses, development partners, and the communities they serve — Africa 63 works where strategy meets execution. From resource mobilisation to governance reform, we help institutions improve their performance, efficiency, and long-term sustainability.

Our approach is deliberate, evidence-led, and partnership-first. We do not chase headlines. We build the relationships, structures, and credibility on which lasting outcomes depend.
